The Wiring Triad

Creativity (Black/Hot) • Power (White/Neutral) • Grounding (Green/Ground)

Creativity – Black/Hot Wire

The active current: source of novelty, disruption, energy, forward drive. High-voltage ideas, art, rebellion, innovation from the edges.

Alone: volatile, burns out fast.

Power – White/Neutral Wire

The return path: institutions, capital, structures, scaling mechanisms, enforcement. Channels energy into sustained output and legacy.

Alone: rigid, stagnant, blocks new flow.

Grounding – Green/Ground Wire

The safety reference: care, mediation, ethics, community anchors, emotional stability, translation between parts. Prevents shocks, dissipates excess.

Alone: no leverage, inaction.

Core Principle: No wire exists in isolation. A complete circuit requires all three connected. Imbalance = failure: burnout, stagnation, exploitation, collapse.

The Wiring Triad

Creativity, Power, and Grounding in Society

The Interdependent Circuit

SYSTEM

BLACK

Creative Innovation

The hot wire — the source of energy, novelty, cultural momentum, artistic expression, intellectual disruption, and generative resistance. Often born from constraint, margins, or refusal.

WHITE

Institutional Power

The neutral/return path — policy, capital allocation, infrastructure, legal frameworks, legacy institutions, enforcement mechanisms. Historically shaped by accumulation and systemic advantage.

GREEN

Grounding & Stabilization

The safety/ground wire — mediators, caregivers, translators, community anchors, bridge-builders, support systems. Prevents shocks, maintains coherence, enables flow between disconnected parts.

Maximize efficiency by using Side Tabs for broad categories and Top Tabs for specific sub-documents.

  • Marriage Licenses

Financial & Legal

  • Immunization Records

Emergency Logistics

  • Evacuation Maps
  • Family Communication Plan
  • Emergency Cash & USB Backups

2. Assembly Checklist

  • Binder: 2-inch heavy-duty D-ring binder (preferably bright red).
  • Protection: Plastic sheet protectors for all original documents.
  • Security: Store in a fireproof/waterproof safe when not in use.
  • Digital: Encrypted USB drive with scans of all paper contents.
